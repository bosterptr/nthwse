How It Works

Dradis CE is an extensible, cross-platform, open source security project framework for collaboration and reporting that’ll save you hours on every project. Built by the best minds in security for forward thinking InfoSec professionals.

Create Reports with One Click

Combine the output of different security scanning tools, manual findings, and notes to generate consistent reports in a fraction of the time of writing them manually.

Collaborate Seamlessly

Track the progress of your project, split tasks, and comment on findings with other team members. Centralized project details keeps everyone on the same page.

Extensible

Dradis connects with 19+ different tools including Burp, Nessus, Nmap, Qualys. And if we don't have an integration for your favorite tool, it's easy to create one!

License and source code

“Free software” means software that respects users' freedom and community. Roughly, it means that the users have the freedom to run, copy, distribute, study, change and improve the software. Thus, “free software” is a matter of liberty, not price.

To understand the concept, you should think of “free” as in “free speech,” not as in “free beer”. We sometimes call it “libre software,” borrowing the French or Spanish word for “free” as in freedom, to show we do not mean the software is gratis.

Dradis Community Edition (CE) is "Free software" and it's also gratis.

We release Dradis CE under GPLv2 and your can find the source on Github: /dradis/dradis-ce

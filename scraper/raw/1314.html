Your all-in-one storage, management and distribution solution for your content.

Gofile offers a range of solutions related to storage, distribution, and data management. Whether you're looking to store data long term, collaborate in a team, distribute to a wide audience, or implement a storage system for your application, Gofile has you covered.
Store

Store your content temporarily for free, or for the long-term with our resilient multi-region storage at a very low cost. We offer one of the lowest storage rates on the market at just $3 per TB per month.

Distribute

Distribute your content directly from our website to a select few friends or colleagues, or share it with millions, all for free. Additionally, seamlessly integrate your content into your own websites or applications with our market-leading CDN service, offering the best value with prices starting as low as $2 per TB of traffic.

Manage

Optimize your content management with our advanced file manager. It provides helpful features like multi-accounts, search, tagging, detailed descriptions, password protection, assignable expiration dates, customizable permissions, and much more.

API

Benefit from our powerful API that can be customized and automated to cater to your specific file management and data handling needs. Every feature of Gofile is accessible from the API, making it possible to efficiently manage your Gofile usage from an external application or website.

No registration required

You can use Gofile immediately, without using an account. Optional sign-in is available for better content management. We offer a multi-account feature for managing multiple accounts simultaneously.

Secure

Your data is replicated across multiple servers and can be accessed from multiple regions. Additionally, the transfer of your files is encrypted according to the latest encryption standards.

Easy to use

Gofile is designed for ease and speed of use. You can upload a file with just one click, but it also allows advanced usage of its features. Its interface is user-friendly, suitable for both beginners and expert users.

Under Development

Gofile is currently in BETA and still under development. Some features may not be available to the general public just yet. If you have any suggestions for future features or improvements, please feel free to contact us.

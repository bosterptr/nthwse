Jakub Domaradzki

Hi there,

My name is Jakub and I'm cybersecurity specialist with strong back-end programming background. I specialize in offensive security performing a wide plethora of activities including (but not limited to): web application pentests, ERP system pentests, source code reviews, infrastructure testing, red teaming etc.

 

In my free time I love to take part in CTFs and some bug bounty programs. Outside of cybersecurity activities, You can most probably find me scuba diving, flying a (virtual) plane, or reading a good book.

 

Pleasure to meet You!

Area of Expertise

Web application/ERP system pentest

No matter if We're talking small web app, or gigantic ERP system - I can perform a simulated attack, and come back with professional report, pointing out vulnerabilities and potential risks. Everything according to known security standards, using self-developed and public tools, as well as manual inspection.

Red Teaming

If Your aim is to check not only systems and protocols that Your organization have in place, but also the people behind them, red teaming is the way to go. From phishing to breaching active directory domain controllers - You set the goal, and I'll show how to achieve it!

Teaching security

It is a great passion of mine to teach cybersecurity to others ❤️ From a small group of developers hearing about Secure Development Lifecycle, to large non-technical staff that needs teaching about what 'phishing' is - everyone deserves to learn!

Weaponizing exploits

I always try to 'break things on the internet' (ie. find vulnerabilities), and then code the most automated way to recreate all the necessary steps to exploit them. Have any fun project I can help with? Please, do let me know.

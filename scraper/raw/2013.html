💡 Search tips

🔸 You can expand your search by using the OR operator.

E.G: brazil OR brasil

🔸Group multiple terms or search operators to control how the search is executed.

E.G: (brazil OR brasil) Rio

🔸Exclude word adding a minus sign in front of words related to the meaning you want to avoid.

E.G: brazil -rio

🔸You can search for an exact phrase by enclosing the phrase in quotation marks.

E.G: "Brazil Digital Report"

🔸Restricts the search to specific file types.

E.G: *.rar

🔸Restricts the search to files that contain the search word in the titles or body text of the documents.

E.G: intext:brazil
